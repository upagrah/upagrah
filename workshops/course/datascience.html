F.A.Q

  • Who can take this workshop?

    Currently we are offering this workshop only in ON PREMISES mode. Hence any academic institution / industry can request for a workshop where our trainees will discuss and plan the sessions according to the availability and feasibility of trainees.

    If you want to request this workshop at your location , please contact us (details in the section below)

  • Are there any pre-requisites for this workshop?

    Any person with the basic knowledge of programming and maths can learn from this course. All that we recommend is interest in the subject ;).

  • Will the participants receive certificates?

    YES. A Certificate of Achievement will be awarded to the trainee on the completion of workshop & post-workshop assesment by the trainer. The descision finally rests in the hands of the trainer of the course. The Certificate will be issued by Upagrah Labs and other Course partners involved at the time of the Course.

  • Will the coordinators receive certificates?

    YES. The coordinators will also recieve a Certificate of co-ordination from Upagrah Labs.

  • Can we conduct this workshop as per our convenience?

    Absolutely! You may conduct the workshop as per your lab availability.
